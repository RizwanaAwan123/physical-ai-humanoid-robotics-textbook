## Chapter 4: Sensing and Perception

Upon completing this chapter, students will be able to:

*   **Identify and describe** various proprioceptive and exteroceptive sensors used in humanoids.
*   **Understand the principles** behind tactile and auditory sensing.
*   **Apply sensor fusion techniques** to integrate multi-modal sensor data.
*   **Explain fundamental computer vision algorithms** relevant to humanoid perception.
*   **Process and interpret sensor data** for state estimation and environment mapping.
