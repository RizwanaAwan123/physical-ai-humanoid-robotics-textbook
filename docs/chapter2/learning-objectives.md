## Chapter 2: Essential Mathematics for Robotics

Upon completing this chapter, students will be able to:

*   **Apply linear algebra concepts** for representing robot poses and transformations.
*   **Utilize calculus** for analyzing robot motion and optimizing control strategies.
*   **Understand quaternions** for efficient 3D rotation representation.
*   **Incorporate probabilistic methods** to handle sensor noise and uncertainty.
*   **Grasp the mathematical foundations** of feedback control systems.
