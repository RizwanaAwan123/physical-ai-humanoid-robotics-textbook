## Chapter 5: Actuation and Mechanical Design

Upon completing this chapter, students will be able to:

*   **Compare and contrast** different types of actuators for humanoid applications.
*   **Understand the principles** and benefits of compliant actuation.
*   **Analyze gearbox designs** and their impact on robot performance.
*   **Evaluate material choices** for humanoid structures based on strength, weight, and stiffness.
*   **Design basic thermal management** and power distribution schemes.
